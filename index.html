Mad Allen is a self-proclaimed artist, whose fusion style is a jumbled concoction of seemingly mismatched musical genres that confound and baffle listeners. Some have described his music as a trainwreck, while others have labeled it as avant-garde or "nice", though it's hard to say which is more accurate. Despite this, or perhaps because of it, he manages to create a strange connection with his audience, leaving some moved and inspired while others scratch their heads in confusion.

With each performance, Mad Allen pushes the boundaries of what's considered acceptable in music, elevating his craft to an art form that can only be described as perplexing. His music is a testament to his unwavering commitment to something, although it's not entirely clear what that something is, but it's there.

Mad Allen's style is certainly different, but whether it's unique and groundbreaking is up for debate. Some have called him a master of his craft, while others have dismissed him as a hack with an inflated ego. One thing's for certain: his performances are not to be missed if you want to have an experience. Send emails to him for good (musical) times or text/phone him at (778) 939-6288.
